Understanding Cancer

Uncontrolled Cell Growth

Cancer occurs when abnormal cells multiply rapidly.

Tumor Formation

Abnormal cells accumulate forming masses.

Metastasis

Cancer can spread to other organs.

Breast Cancer

  • Lump or thickening in breast
  • Change in breast shape
  • Nipple discharge
  • Skin redness
  • Breast pain

Cervical Cancer

  • Abnormal bleeding
  • Pain during intercourse
  • Unusual discharge
  • Pelvic pain

Detection Methods

Clinical Examination

Doctors check for abnormalities.

Mammography

X-ray imaging to detect breast cancer.

Pap Smear

Detects cervical cancer early.
